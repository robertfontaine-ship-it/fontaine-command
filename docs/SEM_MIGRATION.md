## Verification levels

- **Exact lesson document:** a numbered lesson document exists and confirms lesson metadata.
- **Pacing and resource supported:** the lesson sequence is supported by the pacing/learning-target sources and one or more matching classroom resources.
- **Pacing supported:** the sequence is supported by the curriculum sources, but the full lesson package still needs to be built.

These levels prevent a lesson from being labeled complete merely because its title appears in the seed calendar.
